REAL TALK RADIO

This was a summer UROP with Poetic Justice Group at the MIT Media Lab. I created database of over 500 song clips of spoken intros, outros, interludes, and skits in Black music. Then, I made a generative broadcasting system to stream and crossfade the clips.
